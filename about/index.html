The core of the work, in terms of Analytics, Data Visualization and development was performed in Python, Matplotlib and Pandas.
In order to share information with collaborators, routines were developed to export the needed data in the desired formats (docx, xlsx, pdf). Excel was also used extensively to develop reports and other project management tools through the use of pivot tables.

The data were monitored, analyzed, interpreted and used to enable evidence-based decision making on a daily basis, both with regard to my personal work, as well as to support the work of colleagues.
Last but not least, this website is the result of a full-stack project in Vue.js, a Javascript framework.
